# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) - HR Analytics Platform

## General Questions

### What is the HR Analytics Platform?

The HR Analytics Platform is a comprehensive web-based application designed to help organizations analyze, manage, and forecast HR metrics. It provides real-time dashboards, advanced analytics, and predictive insights for workforce management.

### What browsers are supported?

The platform supports:
- Chrome (latest version)
- Firefox (latest version)
- Safari (latest version)
- Edge (latest version)

We recommend using the latest version of your browser for the best experience.

### Is the platform mobile-friendly?

Yes, the platform is fully responsive and works on:
- Desktop computers
- Tablets
- Mobile phones

The interface automatically adapts to your screen size.

### What languages does the platform support?

The platform supports:
- English (US)
- Arabic (Saudi Arabia)

You can switch languages anytime in your preferences.

## Account & Access

### How do I reset my password?

1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Click "Send Reset Link"
4. Check your email for the reset link
5. Click the link and enter your new password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact your administrator.

### How long is my session active?

Your session remains active for 30 minutes of inactivity. After 30 minutes without any action, you'll be automatically logged out for security.

To maintain your session:
- Keep your browser tab active
- Perform actions regularly
- Log in again if you're logged out

### Can I have multiple sessions open?

No, you can only have one active session at a time. If you log in from another device, your previous session will be terminated.

### What should I do if I forget my username?

Contact your system administrator. They can help you recover your username or create a new account.

### How do I change my password?

1. Click your profile icon (top right)
2. Select "Preferences"
3. Click "Change Password"
4. Enter your current password
5. Enter your new password
6. Click "Save"

## Dashboard & Analytics

### Why are my KPI numbers different from last time?

KPI numbers update in real-time as data changes. If you see different numbers:
- New employees may have been added
- Employees may have changed status
- Performance ratings may have been updated
- Leave status may have changed

Check the trend indicator to see if the change is up or down.

### How often is the data updated?

The dashboard updates in real-time as changes occur. You should see updates within 500 milliseconds of a change being made.

### Can I export dashboard data?

Yes, you can export data in multiple formats:
1. Navigate to the dashboard section
2. Click "Export"
3. Choose format: PDF, Excel, or CSV
4. Click "Export"

### How do I filter employees?

1. Click the "Filters" button
2. Select filter criteria (department, status, etc.)
3. Click "Apply Filters"
4. To save filters for later, click "Save Filter Preset"

### What does the trend indicator mean?

The trend indicator shows the change from the previous period:
- Green up arrow: Increase (positive)
- Red down arrow: Decrease (negative)
- The percentage shows the magnitude of change

### How do I search for a specific employee?

1. Click the search box
2. Type the employee name or ID
3. Results update as you type

For advanced search, use field-specific syntax:
- `department:Sales` - Find employees in Sales
- `status:Active` - Find active employees
- Combine criteria: `department:Sales status:Active`

## Reports & Exports

### How do I generate a report?

1. Navigate to "Reports" → "Generate Report"
2. Select report type
3. Choose date range
4. Select filters (optional)
5. Click "Generate"

### What report formats are available?

You can export reports in:
- PDF (for printing and sharing)
- Excel (for data analysis)
- CSV (for importing to other systems)

### How long does report generation take?

Most reports generate within 5 seconds. Large reports with extensive data may take longer.

### Can I schedule reports?

Currently, reports are generated on-demand. You can save reports for later reference.

### How do I save a report?

1. After generating a report, click "Save Report"
2. Enter a report name
3. Click "Save"

Saved reports can be accessed from "Reports" → "Saved Reports"

### Can I export reports in Arabic?

Yes, reports automatically export in your selected language. Arabic reports include proper RTL formatting.

### How do I share a report?

1. Generate or open a saved report
2. Click "Share"
3. Choose sharing method:
   - Email
   - Download link
   - Print
4. Follow the prompts

## Performance & Hiring Forecasts

### How accurate are the hiring forecasts?

Forecasts are based on historical data and current trends. Accuracy depends on:
- Data quality and completeness
- Historical patterns
- External factors (market conditions, company growth)

Each forecast includes a confidence level (0-100%) indicating reliability.

### Can I adjust forecast parameters?

Yes, you can adjust parameters to see different scenarios:
1. Navigate to "Hiring Forecast"
2. Click "Adjust Parameters"
3. Modify growth rate, turnover assumptions, etc.
4. Click "Recalculate"

### What factors influence turnover predictions?

Turnover predictions consider:
- Historical turnover rates
- Department-specific trends
- Role-specific patterns
- Seasonal variations
- External market factors

### How is performance rating calculated?

Performance ratings are based on:
- Manager evaluations
- Key performance indicators (KPIs)
- Peer feedback
- Historical performance trends

Contact your HR department for specific rating criteria.

## Real-Time Features

### Why is my data not updating?

1. Check your internet connection
2. Look for the connection status indicator (top right)
3. If disconnected, the app will sync when reconnected
4. Try refreshing the page

### What happens if I lose internet connection?

The platform queues your changes locally. When your connection is restored:
1. Changes are automatically synced to the server
2. You'll see a notification when sync is complete
3. Data is reconciled with server state

### How do I know if I'm connected?

Check the connection indicator in the top right corner:
- Green dot: Connected
- Yellow dot: Reconnecting
- Red dot: Disconnected

### Can multiple users edit the same data?

Yes, the platform supports concurrent editing. If conflicts occur:
1. You'll receive a notification
2. The server version takes precedence
3. Your local changes are preserved in the history

## Accessibility

### Is the platform accessible for people with disabilities?

Yes, the platform meets WCAG 2.2 Level AA accessibility standards:
- Full keyboard navigation
- Screen reader compatible
- High contrast support
- Text resizable up to 200%

### How do I navigate using only the keyboard?

- `Tab`: Move to next element
- `Shift+Tab`: Move to previous element
- `Enter`/`Space`: Activate buttons
- `Arrow Keys`: Navigate lists
- `Escape`: Close modals

### What screen readers are supported?

The platform works with:
- NVDA (Windows)
- JAWS (Windows)
- VoiceOver (Mac)
- TalkBack (Android)
- VoiceOver (iOS)

### How do I resize text?

Use your browser's zoom feature:
- `Ctrl+` (increase)
- `Ctrl+-` (decrease)
- `Ctrl+0` (reset)

Or adjust text size in browser settings.

## Internationalization

### How do I change the language?

1. Click your profile icon (top right)
2. Select "Preferences"
3. Choose language: English or العربية (Arabic)
4. Click "Save"

The interface updates immediately.

### Does RTL (Right-to-Left) work properly?

Yes, when you select Arabic, the entire interface switches to RTL layout:
- Text direction reverses
- Navigation moves to the right
- Icons flip appropriately

### Are dates formatted correctly for my locale?

Yes, dates and numbers are formatted according to your selected language:
- English: MM/DD/YYYY
- Arabic: DD/MM/YYYY

### Can I export in different languages?

Yes, reports export in your selected language with proper formatting and translations.

## Security & Privacy

### Is my data secure?

Yes, the platform uses:
- HTTPS/TLS 1.2+ encryption
- AES-256 encryption for sensitive data
- Secure token storage
- Content Security Policy (CSP)

### What data is logged?

All user actions are logged for audit purposes:
- Login/logout
- Data viewed
- Data edited
- Data exported
- Reports generated

Logs are retained for 7 years.

### Can I see what data is logged about me?

Yes, contact your administrator to request your audit log.

### How is my password stored?

Passwords are:
- Never stored in plain text
- Hashed using industry-standard algorithms
- Never transmitted in plain text
- Protected by HTTPS encryption

### What is the password policy?

Passwords must:
- Be at least 8 characters long
- Include uppercase letters
- Include numbers
- Include special characters
- Be changed every 90 days

### Is two-factor authentication available?

Yes, if enabled by your administrator. Contact them to enable 2FA for your account.

## Troubleshooting

### The page is loading slowly

1. Check your internet connection
2. Close other browser tabs
3. Clear browser cache
4. Disable browser extensions
5. Try a different browser

### I see an error message

1. Note the error message and code
2. Try refreshing the page
3. Clear browser cache and cookies
4. Log out and log back in
5. Contact support if the error persists

### The search function isn't working

1. Clear the search box

3. Check that filters aren't too restrictive
4. Refresh the page

### Charts aren't displaying

1. Check your internet connection
2. Ensure JavaScript is enabled
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try a different browser
5. Contact support

### Export isn't working

1. Ensure you have sufficient disk space
2. Check browser download settings
3. Try a different export format
4. Disable browser extensions
5. Try a different browser

### I can't see all columns in the table

1. Scroll horizontally to see more columns
2. Adjust column widths by dragging column borders
3. Use the column visibility settings
4. Try a larger screen or zoom out
